Fluke Ball

The microwave at the office is on the fritz… sparks, strange noises, the whole bit. While most people would unplug it and throw it away, these employees keep it around and make games out of it.

Fluke Ball is a turn-based skill/strategy game built around a custom physics engine. Players take turns throwing common office objects toward the microwave and the mysterious phenomenon that surrounds it. Objects still contained in the sphere at the end of the round are scored, and a winner is declared.

Key features:

  • 6 eccentric playable characters
  • 3 game modes
  • Challenging AI
  • Unique, intuitive interface
  • Tournament mode to unlock features
  • Hidden “unlock code” for those who just want to play

Credits:

  • Game: Andrew Paradise
  • Art and characters: Georg Pedersen

The game is hosted by jayisgames.com as an entry into their 4th Casual Game Design Competition. You can go there for discussion about the game, and of course to play it!
